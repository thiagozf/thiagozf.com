# Hi, I'm Thiago! 👋

I'm a brazilian software engineer born in 1993. I went to
[UFSC](https://ufsc.br) where I graduated with a Bachelor degree in Computer
Engineering, with emphasis on Embedded Systems Development.

I've written code for companies like [Way](https://way.live) and
[Betha Sistemas](http://betha.com.br/), and also have
[my open source code](https://tzf.im/github) being used by some people all over
the globe.

This website is my
[digital garden](https://tomcritchlow.com/2019/02/17/building-digital-garden/).
It's a spot where I post ideas, snippets, resources, thoughts, collections, and
other things that I find interesting or useful. Expect to find content about
JavaScript, Serverless, Linux and other fun stuff here. This is also a way to
exercise and improve my english writing. ~~Plus, coding your own blog is also
kind of cool too.~~

You can also find more content written by yours truly on other platforms,
including [Twitter](https://tzf.im/twitter).
